Oracle

The Polybet DAO is more than a governance tool. It is a decentralized betting oracle acting as the supreme authority approving bet outcomes, ensuring fairness, transparency, and accuracy.

A DAO-based approach allows public betting events to be decided on a transparent and fair manner to ensure that all results are verified by oracles independently. And wining amounts are distributed automatically via smart contract execution.

Public Event Oracle Workflow

Public event outcomes are submitted to the DAO oracle for approval.

The event outcome is approved and based on community consensus.

The event outcomes are determined and the winner is selected.

Community Consensus Through DAO Voting

Token holders participate in DAO voting on event outcomes.

Integrity is ensured through decentralized consensus.

The community consensus determines bet outcomes and the winner.

Oracle-Approved Results Returned 
to the Platform

DAO voting approves the event outcome.

Approved results are returned to Polybet.

Smart contract distribute winning amounts. In a transparent manner.

Stake Govern Enjoy!

PLBT governance token lies at the heart of the platform. PLBT token holders have the right to participate in DAO voting and receive diverse rewards.

100% of commissions is distributed as rewards to users.

Vote and Earn

Holders of at least 2 PLBT tokens can vote on the outcomes.
A commission of 20% from every bet is distributed as rewards to users who voted for the winning outcome of an event.

Stake and Earn

PLBT token can be staked. 35% of all commissions received by the platform to be distributed to all PLBT token holders as rewards.

A synthetic sPLBT token is issued to stakers for participating in DAO voting on platform development, new functionality proposals, and commission distribution schemes.

*Rewards are credited daily in USDT to staker balance accounts only.

Referral Program

Boost your earnings on Polybet with the referral program to get up to 30% of invited user commissions and 5% from tier 2 users.

The referral program is suitable for news sites, channels, public pages, as well as influencers and affiliates. It will be possible to monetize news traffic by inviting users to place a bet on newsworthy event in the world.

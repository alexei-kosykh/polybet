Oracle

The Polybet DAO is more than a governance instrument – it is a decentralized betting oracle acting as the supreme authority determining bet outcomes, ensuring fairness, transparency, and accuracy.

A DAO-based approach allows public bets to be reviewed in a transparent and fair manner to ensure that all results are verified by oracles independently and winnings are distributed automatically via smart contract execution.
